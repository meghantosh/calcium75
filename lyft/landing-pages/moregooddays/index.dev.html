#MoreGoodDays

For breast cancer patients, dependable transportation to and from treatment is often a challenge; some are not allowed to drive themselves (doctor’s orders), and taking public transportation may not be an option. That’s where Lyft and Ford Warriors in Pink® come in.

We’ve teamed up with Ford Warriors in Pink® to give up to 10,000 free rides to breast cancer patients traveling to and from treatment.

Help Us Find 10,000 Ways to Have a Good Day: Share whatever helps you turn a difficult day into a better one — from a walk in the sunshine, your favorite funny video, to a phone call with your best friend — on Twitter, Instagram, or Facebook, and include #MoreGoodDays. For each good day tip you share, Ford Warriors in Pink® will donate up to 10,000 Lyft rides to breast cancer patients in need. The more tips you share, the more good days we can give to those who really need one. And, your photos and tips will act as an inspiration bank on how to make their day a bit better.

Ford Warriors in Pink®

For more than 20 years, Ford Motor Company has worked to advance the conversation around breast cancer – inspiring the next generation of warriors in the fight, while continuing to fuel the spirit of those living with the disease. To date, Ford has contributed more than $128 million to the cause.

As a member of the Army National Guard, Gene’s had some tough workdays in his life. But it’s raising his son as a single father that he says is the toughest job of all — and the most rewarding.

To make ends meet, last year Gene took an evening job as a security guard for the state. He was getting home past 9 p.m., making it difficult to find a steady babysitter or help his son with homework. Something had to give. For Gene, the decision was easy.
