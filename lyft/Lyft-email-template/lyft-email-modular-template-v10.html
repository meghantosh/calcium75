Your Mission: Give 30 Rides in 30 Days
 
Perks for completing your 30th ride:
  • You'll be graduated to Lyft Veteran  status, saving yourself a spot in the top-secret Driver Lounge for Santa Barbara.
  • We'll ship you a Welcome Kit, which includes your very own pink 'stache – the symbol of our movement, and of your achievement.
  • You'll be on your way to Lyft MVP  status, which includes access to special events and other great perks once you hit 100 rides.
 
More Tips to Help You Get Started
 
Earn more by minding the hot zones!
Take a look at the Santa Barbara Coverage Map . Be sure you're in the coverage area, then head to a hot zone to pick up passengers.

Get a phone charger for your car. 
We recommend getting a USB phone charger so your phone doesn't die while you're driving. If you don't have a charger, purchase one online or at any major gas station. Also pick up a phone mount for hands-free navigation.

Check out the Driver FAQ. 
Check out our detailed Driver FAQ  to learn more, or ask a question in the  Santa Barbara Driver Lounge on Facebook. For urgent issues while you're on the road, hit the support button in the top corner of the Lyft app.

And don't forget to have fun!
Give passengers a fistbump, start a conversation, and flash that beautiful smile of yours. You're about to make a lot of new friends.
